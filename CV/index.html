Personal Statement

Highly motivated individual with an analytical mindset and a passion for all fields of motorsport. Possessing a strong foundation in coding and mathematics plus ten years of Motocross racing experience across North America where I developed hands on experience in racing strategy, equipment maintenance, and testing new bike setups.
